I am a Research Engineer at [GAC R&D Center](https://www.gacrnd.com/) working on learning-based planning system for autonomous driving. My work combines robot learning, decision-making, and large-scale data processing pipeline.

I received my Master's degree in Mechanical Engineering from the [National University of Singapore](https://cde.nus.edu.sg/me/). My research interests lie in robot learning and autonomous systems, particularly at the intersection of model-based methods, imitation learning, and reinforcement learning. My long-term goal is to build data–algorithm flywheels for autonomous systems, enabling robots to continuously improve through experience and acquire robust, adaptable, and physically grounded behaviors.
